About Me

Muhammad Fakhrudin ( Fakhru ) is an Embedded System and Software Engineering enthusiast with years of experience since he studies an electrical engineering in 2012 at Telkom University. He starts his journey as a robot enthusiast at his mini project using C++ Programming Language. He keeps learning about Programming and Electronics when doing group projects and the final project at Telkom University in his bachelor’s degree program.

In 2020, He start master’s degree in Microelectronics Engineering at Bandung Institute of Technology (ITB). His Thesis about Artificial Intelligence algorithm that applied in path-planning system then design accelerator hardware using Field-Programmable Gate Array. He graduated from ITB in 2023 and focused on pursuing his personal goal which is became a microelectronics engineer and explore as a software engineer.
